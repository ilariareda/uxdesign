IKIGUIDE | Travel Agency

Naming concept: Ikiguide (from the japanese Ikigai

Ikiguide is a potential travel agency that I created as a project.

Role: This is my first Graphic Design project, it is about creating a travel agency and deliver the final product including it's concept, brand identity and social media.

Duration: 7-8 weeks.

WHAT IS IKIGUIDE?

Ikiguide is a travel agency that offers clients the opportunity to go on a journey of self-discovery and self-exploration while traveling sustainably throughout stunning places. They believe in the power of nature, yoga and hiking as essential healing elements and for the discovery of the ikigai, that is everyone’s reason for living.

BRAND NAME

WHY Ikiguide?

The name Ikiguide comes from the combination of the worlds IKIGAI and GUIDE. Guide clearly refers to a travel agency while IKIGAI is a famous japonese concept which means: the reason of being or what gets you up in the morining. The idea is that, by traveling in a certain way, doing yoga and being in the nature, the clients can find their purpose in life. Because of this, the subtitle is Travel with Purpose.

LOGO DESIGN

The logo is simple. It has a meaningful letter, the third “I”. The letter I holds the other symbol, that looks like the world but also represents the Ikigai symbol, since it is formed by four circles that cross each other. The logo contains the four colors that are used throughout the whole design.

TYPOGRAPHY

ORGANIC DESIGN

Shapes, lines and colors follow an organic design in order to evoke a sense of being immersed in nature. The colors are natural and earth colors. They give a grounding and calming feeling.
